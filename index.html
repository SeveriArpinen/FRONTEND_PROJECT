Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy if I accidentally deleted something important in the app?

No worries! Our app has an undo feature, and we also offer a cloud backup to help you recover lost data. If it's really gone, contact our support team, and we'll do our best to help you out.

Can I use this app on a space station, or is Earth-only for now?

While we’re not quite ready for space travel, as long as you have internet access on Earth (or potentially beyond), the app should work wherever you are!

Can the app help me become more productive, or should I just accept my fate of endless procrastination?

While we can't wave a magic wand to make procrastination disappear, the app has features like reminders, task lists, and progress tracking that can help you get back on track. We believe in your potential to conquer distractions!

What’s the best way to store my data if I’m running out of storage on my device?

We recommend using cloud storage, which is easily accessible through the app. You can back up your data automatically so that your device doesn’t run out of space while you keep everything safe and secure!

Can I just leave a review using emojis instead of words?

We love emojis and they definitely add character! However, for reviews to be most helpful to other users, we recommend using a few words along with your emojis. It’s a win-win!
